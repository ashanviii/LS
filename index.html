Liquidity
Investors can invest by simply purchasing a token which represents a stake in the equity of a given project. The token is immediately transferred to their secured wallet and can be traded at any time on secondary market, after the anti-speculation period is over, allowing increased liquidity, something unprecedented in the real estate investment sector.
Transparency
Investors who acquire project tokens become part of the project's tracking channel, receiving regular updates via a user-friendly smartphone app. Quarterly in-person site visits are also offered to preserve the tangible aspect of investments.
Diversification
Our platform allow investors to browse through our bespoke of alternative investment propositions and pick those in which they believe, and most importantly those which fit their risk-return profile.
Safety

To enhance investor confidence:

  • Funds are held in escrow until the fundraising target is met, with instant equity refunds if not.
  • Developers are rigorously vetted for their track record.
  • After the project ends, a smart contract automatically distributes funds recovered by the SPV to investors and destroys tokens.

SOURCING
Investment managers submit their investment projects and after thorough due diligence we make it possible for you to become their limited partner
FUNDRAISING
A timeframe is set to collect the targeted amount of equity - until the goal is reached, your money stays in an escrow and will be sent back to your account if the fundraising is not conclusive.
LAUNCH
The investment manager collects its equity, raises debt from banks and kicks off his development project. You will receive regular updates on your dashboard and you will be able to regularly visit the asset
EXIT

Choose between two options:

    1. Wait until completion of the project and get your stake of the profits - which will be automatically sent to your account through the smart contract integrated on the blockchain
      2. Choose to cash out earlier through the secondary exchange market by reselling your stake in project
